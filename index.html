Nestled beside a peaceful loch and surrounded by towering pines, Lochquarry Outdoor Centre began as a simple dream:to give families a place to reconnect — not just with nature, but with each other. for years, our founders brought their own children here to paddle across the lake, cook over open fires, and climb the rugged hills. With each visit, they saw something amazing — laughter, courage, teamwork, and memories that lasted far beyond the weekend.That is when they knew it was time to share the magic. Today, Lochquarry welcomes families from all over to discover the outdoors like never before. Whether you're learning archery for the first time, kayaking across still waters, or challenging your self on our rope courses, every activity is designed with safety, fun, and family bonding in mind. Come for a day. Stay for the weekend. Leave with a story.

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre Activities

Hillwalking

Hill walking serves as a holistic activity that supports physical health, mental well-being, and relaxation. It provides cardiovascular exercise, strengthens muscles, and enhances balance through movement on varied terrain, while also promoting weight management and respiratory health. Mentally, it reduces stress and anxiety by encouraging mindfulness and exposing individuals to natural environments, which can have a calming effect on the nervous system. As a form of active relaxation, hill walking allows for mental clarity, emotional reset, and a break from digital distractions, making it a powerful practice for both health and rejuvenation .

Water Activities

Water activities, such as swimming, kayaking, or aqua aerobics, offer a dynamic blend of health benefits and relaxation. Physically, they provide low-impact, full-body exercise that enhances cardiovascular fitness, muscle strength, and joint flexibility, making them ideal for people of all ages and fitness levels. The natural resistance of water supports injury prevention and rehabilitation, while its buoyancy reduces strain on the body. Mentally, being in or around water has a calming effect, lowering stress and anxiety levels, and promoting a sense of peace and well-being. As both a fitness and leisure activity, water-based exercise supports holistic health while offering a soothing, meditative experience.

Rope Activities

Rope activities, such as climbing, rope courses, or skipping rope, provide a unique combination of physical conditioning and mental engagement that supports overall health and relaxation. These activities enhance strength, coordination, balance, and cardiovascular endurance, while also improving grip and core stability. Mentally, rope challenges require focus, problem-solving, and confidence-building, which can reduce stress and boost self-esteem. The rhythmic motion of jumping rope or the immersive nature of rope climbing promotes mindfulness and can serve as a healthy outlet for tension. Overall, rope activities offer both invigorating exercise and a satisfying sense of accomplishment that contributes to physical and emotional well-being.
